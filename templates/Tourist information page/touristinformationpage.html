Dynamic Earth



Dynamic earth, a fantastic place to bring the kids to learn about the history of the earth, the ice age, dinosaurs and other extinct animals.
Dynamic earth is fun for adults too, Dynamic Earth provides tours for schools from the UK and abroad with workshops and other activities.
Hungry? Thirsty? Dynamic Earth has its own cafe the "Food Chain Cafe"
Hot food is served until 3pm.
